## Design Context

### Users
- Primary users: potential karyakartas and members arriving at the public landing page.
- Secondary users: internal users who need a fast, visible sign-in path into the ERP.
- Primary job to be done: understand Pragya Pravah's workstreams and institutional seriousness quickly enough to explore further.

### Brand Personality
- Intellectual, authoritative, grounded.
- Serious and institutional rather than playful or startup-like.
- Public-facing movement of ideas with visible operational depth.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Movement and ideas platform, not an ERP splash screen and not a generic NGO homepage.
- Visibly bilingual from the first fold, but with asymmetric language hierarchy rather than full duplication of all copy.
- Editorial and cultural visual language with restrained saffron/earth accents, warm light surfaces, and typography-led hierarchy.
- Abstract cultural/editorial visual treatment instead of a literal institutional photo hero.

### Design Principles
1. Lead with public intellectual work, not software features.
2. Make credibility legible without turning the landing page into a metrics dashboard.
3. Keep `Explore Work` primary and `Sign In` secondary but always visible.
4. Use bilingual hierarchy intentionally: one language leads, the other supports.
5. Favor disciplined editorial composition over decorative app-marketing patterns.
